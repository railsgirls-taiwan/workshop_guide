  8. [CodeSchool: Ruby](http://www.codeschool.com/paths/ruby) (英文, 部份課程需月費)
  9. [Ruby on Rails Tutorial](http://ruby.railstutorial.org/ruby-on-rails-tutorial-book) (英文)
